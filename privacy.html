Privacy Policy

Ride Ready stores the bike maintenance data you enter so you can use the app across your devices. If you connect Strava, Ride Ready also stores the Strava account data needed to identify your bikes and sync their mileage.

What Ride Ready stores

  • Your Strava athlete ID and basic profile information.
  • Your connected Strava bike list and synced mileage data.
  • Your app data, including bikes, wheelsets, tyres, sealant, cassette, brake pad settings, and service history.

How the data is used

The data is used only to run Ride Ready, sync your bikes and ride mileage from Strava, and keep your bike maintenance data available across devices.

Where the data is stored

Ride Ready stores app data in Supabase and uses Strava only for authentication and bike or ride mileage sync.

Sharing

Ride Ready is intended to keep each rider’s data separate. Your Strava and maintenance data should not be visible to other users of the app.

Your control

You can export your app data from Ride Ready at any time. If you stop using the app, disconnecting Strava stops future syncs.

Contact

If you need your data removed, contact the app owner directly before wider public release details are added here.
